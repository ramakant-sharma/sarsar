How to reach

By Bus

Udasar Bidawtan (Udasar) is located in Churu-Sardarhar highway between Churu and Sardarshahar. From Churu and Sardarshahr, the buses are in almost fifteen minutes, so that the Udasar can reach the stand. And there is a taxi service on Udasar that helps us reach Sarsar village.

By Train

There is no railway station in Sarsar. The nearest railway station is Churu junction and Sardarshahar railway station.
