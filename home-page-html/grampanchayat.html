About to Derajsar

According to Census 2011 information the location code or village code of Derajsar village is 070457. Derajsar village is located in Sardarshahar Tehsil of Churu district in Rajasthan, India. It is situated 25km away from sub-district headquarter Sardarshahar and 32km away from district headquarter Churu. As per 2009 stats, Derajsar village is also a gram panchayat. The total geographical area of village is 2982.9 hectares. Derajsar has a total population of 2,681 peoples. There are about 475 houses in Derajsar village. Sardarshahar is nearest town to Derajsar which is approximately 25km away.
